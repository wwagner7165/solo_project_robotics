Robotics Club

In robotitcs club, you'll build, design, and compete with other teams. We meet around 2:15 to 3:30 every week except Fridays and weekends.

VEX Robotics

What is VEX? Well VEX Robotics is a robotics program for elementary through university students, and a subset of Innovation First International.

The Robotics Coach

Mr. Ryan Macomber is Cox Mill's robotics coach in room 1626. He is a Physics teacher, so you'll also learn more about how to apply physics to the real world.

How To Join

  1. Go to room 1626
  2. Talk to Mr. Macomber
  3. Sign a Forum
  4. Have fun
    • You don't have to make it everyday, but try to make it most of the time.
    • You don't have to be an expert to join.

Things To Expect In Robotics

Programming VEX is loosely based on the C language, but takes ideas from C++ as well as the RenderMan shading language.
Building Vex will give you a Brain, tons of metal, and etc. You'll be able to build and design your own robot for competition.
Competition VEX competitions are change annually. So a new game every year where you'll compet with other teams.
